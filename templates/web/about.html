About

Viswamaithri Website accessed through viswamaithrithinks.com is a non-profit organization constituted under the creative supervision of Viswavidya Gurukulam. The prime objective is to put forward creative and productive thinking to be a popular endeavor. The state has to ensure the well-being of all that constitutes its structure. Irrespective of discrepancies based on any cultural practices such as caste, religion, or political lineage all the citizens must be a part of such an initiative. The central ideologies of this community are the humanitarian and ethical values of all religious and cultural philosophies. We encourage an enlightened civilian movement. It should be a mission to seek the voluntary participation of all patriots who respect human values, regardless of caste, creed, or political affiliation. We set forth, propose and contribute to such an endeavour.
